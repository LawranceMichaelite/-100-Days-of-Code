An average person lives about 75-80 years. We spend about 25 years of our life sleeping(excluding only few). That’s a big number which accounts for almost one third of our entire life, but still 30% of us struggle to sleep well. We spend about 8 years of our life in social medias and internet, about 4 years eating and drinking, about 4 years in education, about 2 years grooming(personal care), about 2 years shopping, about 1 year in child care, about 1&1/2 years travelling, about 6 years doing chores and about 10 years working. That leaves us with only about 10 years. How are we going to spend it?.
Everyday 86,400 seconds are deposited into our life account. At the end of the day once they’re all used up, we get another new 86,400 seconds to start a new day.
Likewise, imagine we wake up every day with $86,400 in our bank account, and at the end of the day it’s all gone whether we spent it or not. And then the next day we get another $86,400. What would we do with it? We want to make its best use , Is it? We don’t like to waste if it’s money, so why do we waste it when it comes to time? Time is more precious than those dollars. Those seconds are so much powerful that you can make more dollars but you can’t make more time using dollars.
